Create Some Space

Designate a workspace at home for those late nights. This can be anywhere you have access to a flat surface and adequate lighting that allow you to concentrate-a desk, you kitchen table, a reading chair, just not bed! This help your mind and body understand when you´re in working mode and allows you to more effective transition to "home" mode when yor´re done.
