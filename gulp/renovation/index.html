OUR MISSION

Less Waste

Recycling luxury kitchens, bath fixtures, home furnishings, and Architectural elements diverts millions of pounds from landfills

Create Jobs

The white-glove removal, transportation, and resale of these items creates numerous construction, logistics, and retail jobs as these items are pre-pared for re-purposing.

Support Non-Profits

Proceeds earned from selling recycled luxury creates support for innovative and life-changing outreach programs in the areas of: youth-at-risk, addiction recovery, job creation, and social entrepreneurship.
